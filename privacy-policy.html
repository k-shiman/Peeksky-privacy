Privacy Policy

PeekSky  ·  Last updated: June 18, 2026

Short version: This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

1. What This Extension Does

PeekSky is a Chrome browser extension that displays current weather conditions, a 7-day forecast, hourly forecasts, sport activity suitability ratings, air quality, and sleep quality scores for any city in the world.

2. Data We Collect and How It Is Used

2.1 Location (Geolocation)

When you open the extension for the first time, it may request access to your device's geographic location. This permission is optional — you can deny it and use the city search instead.

If permission is granted, your coordinates are used only to:

  • Identify your nearest city (via OpenStreetMap Nominatim reverse geocoding)
  • Fetch weather data for that city

Your coordinates are never stored on any server, never sold, and never used for advertising or profiling. After the city name is resolved, the coordinates are discarded.

2.2 City Search Queries

When you type a city name in the search field, the text is sent to the Open-Meteo Geocoding API to find matching cities. No personal information is attached to these requests.

2.3 Data Stored Locally (on your device only)

The extension uses your browser's localStorage to remember your preferences between sessions. This data never leaves your device:

  • Last city — city name, country, and coordinates of the last viewed city
  • Recent cities — up to 3 recently searched cities for quick access
  • Temperature unit — your preference (°C or °F)

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for the extension, or by uninstalling the extension.

3. Third-Party APIs

The extension makes requests to the following third-party services to fetch weather and location data. No personal information, user identifiers, or account data is sent to these services — only geographic coordinates or city names.

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Open-Meteo
api.open-meteo.com		 Weather forecast, hourly data, air quality (AQI, pollen) open-meteo.com/en/terms
Open-Meteo Geocoding
geocoding-api.open-meteo.com		 City name search and autocomplete open-meteo.com/en/terms
Nominatim / OpenStreetMap
nominatim.openstreetmap.org		 Reverse geocoding (coordinates → city name) when geolocation is used osmfoundation.org

All three services are open-source or open-data projects. None of them use the data sent by this extension for advertising or user tracking.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No email addresses or names
  • No browsing history or tabs
  • No usage analytics or crash reports
  • No cookies
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data is sold or shared with any third parties for commercial purposes

5. Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The extension contains no age-restricted content.
